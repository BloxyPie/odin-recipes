Pizza Recipe

Description:

Enjoy the perfect harmony of tangy tomato sauce, gooey melted cheese, and your favorite toppings, all baked to perfection on a crispy crust.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Italian seasoning (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza pan or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Spread a layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add your favorite toppings on top of the cheese.
  7. If desired, drizzle a little olive oil over the toppings and sprinkle with minced garlic and Italian seasoning.
  8. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  9. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  10. If desired, sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese and torn fresh basil leaves over the hot pizza before serving.
  11. Slice the pizza into wedges and serve hot. Enjoy!
