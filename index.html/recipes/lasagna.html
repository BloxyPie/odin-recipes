Lasagna Recipe

Description:

Indulge in layers of perfectly cooked lasagna noodles, rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel, and a tantalizing blend of melted cheese, all baked to golden perfection for the ultimate comfort food experience.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Italian seasoning

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, cook ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet with the meat and cook until softened.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce and Italian seasoning. Let simmer for about 10 minutes.
  6. In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese with a portion of shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese.
  7. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  8. Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  9. Spoon a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  10. Repeat the layers (meat sauce, noodles, cheese mixture) until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  11. Sprinkle the remaining shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the top.
  12. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  14. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
